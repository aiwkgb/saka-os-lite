# Code of Conduct

SAKA OS Lite welcomes constructive contributions from beginners and experienced builders.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and practical.
- Help make the project easier to understand.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Protect privacy; never publish someone else's sensitive data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets or private data.
- Spam or misleading claims about adoption/impact.

Maintainers may remove issues, comments, or contributions that violate these expectations.
